Hello and welcome!
My name is Benjamin Cohen, and I am an enthusiastic apprentice Full Stack developer taking my first steps in the exciting world of web development.

BIO

I was born and raised in Israel and now living in Philadelphia, currently a student in Thinkful’s Full Stack program and finding every challenge a new amazing journey. I got into coding for my fondness for creation and all things aesthetic, which I exlpored during my years as a musician, singer-songwriter and artist.

I love seeing how creative ideas materialize and grow into functional, useful products that ultimately enhance and improve our daily lives.
I currently teach as my day job, and so must overcome challenges and come up with elegant solutions on a daily basis.
I drink copious amounts of coffee, listen to music by people who are either at least in their fifties or completely dead, and I painstakingly tweak CSS declarations with the concentration of a Buddhist monk.
I am but a humble, lowly beginner in this fascinating, yet perplexing field of web development, and as such I hope to learn from the best out there and grow as a developer and part of a team.
