Hello and welcome!
My name is Benjamin Cohen, and I am an enthusiastic apprentice Full Stack developer taking my first steps in the exciting world of web development.

BIO

I was born and raised in Israel and now living in Philadelphia, currently a student in Thinkful’s Full Stack program and finding every challenge a new amazing journey. I got into coding for my fondness for creation and all things aesthetic, which I exlpored during my years as a musician, singer-songwriter and artist.
I love seeing how creativity and ingenuity materialize and grow into functional, useful tools that change how we live our lives and how we experience the world
I have a degree in Art History and my first career was in teaching, though my passion from a young age was for creation, be it through an artistic medium or more recemtly in the digital realm. Few things in my life satisfy me more than witnessing a creation of mine take form and in turn make even the slightest positive contribution to someone's day.
I drink copious amounts of coffee, listen to music by people who are either at least in their fifties or completely dead, and achieve Zen by painstakingly tweaking CSS declarations.
Always looking to conquer the next challenge and to be awed and inspired by wiser people than myself.

PORTFOLIO

YOU DON'T KNOW TOM WAITS

You either love him, hate him, or you don’t know Tom Waits. Test your knowledge of the life and work of this otherworldly musical phenomena. Programmed with HTML5, CSS and Javascript.

THE METROPOLITAN MUSEUM ARTWORK FINDER

If you are an art lover, this is the web app for you! With this app you will be able to peruse through the Metropolitan Museum's thousands of paintings and artifacts by simple keyword search. Each image is presented with a detailed description courtesy of the Met museum expansive API. Programmed with HTML5, CSS and Javascript.

  • Live

NATIONAL PARK SEARCH

If you're a fan of the great American outdoors and looking for your next holiday destination, this is the app for you. Simply enter the state you wish to visit and get a comprehensive list of all the national parks of that state with a comprehensive description and relevant links. Developed using jQuery, HTML5, CSS3 and the National Parks Service API.

  • Live

GITHUB REPO BROWSER

This littly handy tool will allow you to browse through any Github user's public repos by entering their username. A little showcase of my simple minimalist design philosophy with an eye toward sleek and simple UI. Developed with HTML5, CSS3, jQuery and the Github API.

  • Live

LIVEMODO

A wiki database for live music aficionados to share their concert experience. Explore new music, find out which acts are worth your time and which are not worth the cover charge. Fully integrated with a backend, you may register, log in, post reviews as a registered user or casually browse posted reviews. Built with ReactJS, CSS3 and Express.
